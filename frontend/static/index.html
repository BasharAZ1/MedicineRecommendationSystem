Medical Topics

We provide insights and solutions for a variety of medical conditions, including:

  • Bacterial Pneumonia: An infection in one or both lungs caused by bacteria. Symptoms include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing.
  • Corona Virus Disease (COVID-19): A highly contagious virus that causes respiratory illness with symptoms ranging from mild to severe, including fever, cough, and shortness of breath.
  • Normal: Refers to the absence of any pathological condition. Maintaining normal health is essential for overall well-being.
  • Tuberculosis (TB): A serious infectious disease that primarily affects the lungs. Symptoms include a persistent cough, chest pain, and coughing up blood.
  • Viral Pneumonia: An infection of the lungs caused by viruses. Symptoms are similar to bacterial pneumonia but may also include a runny nose and muscle pain.

Fracture Status

We offer diagnostic tools and information regarding bone health, including:

  • Fractured: Refers to a break or crack in a bone. Treatment typically involves immobilization, physical therapy, and sometimes surgery.
  • Not Fractured: Indicates that the bone is intact and there is no break or crack. Regular check-ups and a healthy lifestyle help maintain strong bones.
